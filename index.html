Our Story

Built On Discipline, Poured With Purpose

Kinetic Coffee Company was founded by an Air Force veteran who spent his service keeping radio and satellite communications equipment running. Gear that has to work, every time, with zero room for error. Long shift work, odd hours, and a short tour taught him that reliability isn't optional. It's the whole job.

When he got out, he brought that same standard home. Beans sourced deliberately, roasted to order by a trusted partner, and evaluated batch by batch, the same way a piece of comms equipment gets tested and re-tested before anyone trusts it to work.

Every bag is roasted in small batches and shipped out ready to get you moving.

"We don't roast coffee to relax. We roast it to move." — Founder, Kinetic Coffee Co.

Our Roasting Partner

Roasted By Wagon Coffee

Every bag of Kinetic Coffee is roasted by Wagon Coffee Roasters, a Denver based women-owned coffee roastery founded in 2020. Wagon employs and supports women in recovery from addiction, offering real jobs, encouragement, and a second chance through coffee.

Their beans are roasted on an all-electric, zero-emission roaster that cuts the carbon footprint of every batch by roughly 90% compared to a traditional gas roaster.

WOMEN-OWNED & OPERATED

Founded and run by women, employing women in recovery across every stage of the roasting process.

100% ELECTRIC ROASTING

Roasted on a Bellwether roaster — zero emissions, ~90% lower carbon footprint per batch.

SUPPORT SMALL FARMS

Green coffee bought through trusted import partners. You are also directly impacting the livelihoods of small coffee farmers across the globe through our commitment to paying equitable prices to producers and investing in communities where coffee is grown. Wagon Coffee sources from small lot farms that all use environmentally friendly agents to grow their crops. Many of them don't have the ability to certify as "organic" but they are following all the guidelines.
